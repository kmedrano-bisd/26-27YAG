Curriculum Strategy & Context Notes

Because ALL 6th-grade cohorts enter the 7th Honors track directly next year, the entire scope and sequence is unified to prepare students for the advanced pacing.

Timeline Benchmark Key TEKS & Components Strategic YAG Note
Late August - Early Sept SE: 6.7A, 6.7D, 6.4F, 6.3B, 6.3E Heavy focus on distributive properties, GCF, and multiplying/dividing fractions. MATHia is introduced immediately.
October SE: 6.4A, 6.4B, 6.4C, 6.5A Transitioning into ratios, double number lines, and tape diagrams.
December SE: 6.2A, 6.2B, 6.2C, 6.11A Absolute value and extending the coordinate plane. All four quadrants introduced.
March & April Multiple SEs (STAAR Reviews) Massive sequential blocks entirely dedicated to STAAR REVIEW prior to the MATH STAAR in late April.
Late April / May (Bridge) SE: 7.4B, 7.5B, 7.8C, 7.9B, 7.9C "Since ALL 6th enters 7th Honors" - The YAG shifts immediately to 7th grade math standards, including calculating circle area ($A = \pi r^2$).
