Track Divergence & Strategic Notes

Track side-by-side splits where 6th Honors pulls in accelerated 7th grade math targets.

Topic Area Target Dates 6th On-Level Pace 6th Honors Pace Teacher Differentiation Strategy
Factors & Multiples Aug 17 - Sep 8 Focuses on numbers: GCF, LCM, and simple prime values. Moves fast into prime bases with multi-step exponents. Honors will jump to abstract algebraic layouts much sooner than On-Level.
Ratios & Proportions Sep 11 - Oct 8 Uses model layouts: Tape diagrams and double number lines. Moves directly into 7th Grade constant rates ($y=kx$). On-Level requires visual models; Honors needs function equations and slope connections.
The Coordinate Plane Dec 1 - Dec 16 Focuses mostly on Quadrant 1, moving into matching reflections. Full 4-quadrant manipulation, including geometric coordinate distance. Ensure Honors connects graphing solutions smoothly back to inequality constraints.
Area & Geometry Structures Mar 10 - Apr 1 Decomposes base polygons (triangles, rectangles, trapezoids). Pulls in 7th grade circle area ($A = \pi r^2$) and circumference tracking. On-Level works with straight lines; Honors handles curves, pi, and radius metrics.
Linear Relationships & Functions Jan 6 - Feb 25 Identifies input/output rows and additive vs multiplicative scales ($y=x+b$; $y=kx$). Calculates non-proportional graphs ($y=mx+b$) and solves equations with variables on both sides. Honors completely skips simple practice loops to establish multi-step linear algebra.
Proportional Scaling & Angles May 11 - May 14 Identifies matching scale elements visually across simple grid images. Applies fractional scale factors to geometric blueprints and calculates adjacent angle rules. On-Level builds baseline definitions; Honors immediately targets composite structural configurations.
Probability Theory May 17 - May 20 Defines raw chance metrics using primary fractions. Calculates tree diagrams, sample spaces, and multi-event compound odds. Honors moves straight to mathematical predictions using theoretical rules.
