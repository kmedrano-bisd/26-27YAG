Track Divergence & Strategic Notes

Track side-by-side splits where 6th Honors pulls in accelerated 7th grade math targets.

Topic Area 6th On-Level Pace 6th Honors Pace Teacher Differentiation Strategy
Factors & Multiples Focuses on numbers: GCF, LCM, and simple prime values. Moves fast into prime bases with multi-step exponents. Honors will jump to abstract algebraic layouts much sooner than On-Level.
Ratios & Proportions Uses model layouts: Tape diagrams and double number lines. Moves directly into 7th Grade constant rates ($y=kx$). On-Level requires visual models; Honors needs function equations and slope connections.
The Coordinate Plane Focuses mostly on Quadrant 1, moving into matching reflections. Full 4-quadrant manipulation, including geometric coordinate distance. Ensure Honors connects graphing solutions smoothly back to inequality constraints.
Area & Geometry Structures Decomposes base polygons (triangles, rectangles, trapezoids). Pulls in 7th grade circle area ($A = \pi r^2$) and circumference tracking. On-Level works with straight lines; Honors handles curves, pi, and radius metrics.
